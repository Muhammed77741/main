## ðŸ“ Scoring System Explained

**Total Score: 0-100 points**

### 1. Momentum (0-30 points)
- Price performance (1M, 3M, 6M returns)
- Trend consistency (higher highs, higher lows)
- Acceleration (is momentum increasing?)

### 2. Relative Strength (0-25 points)
- Performance vs S&P500
- Outperformance over 1M and 3M
- Market leadership

### 3. Trend Quality (0-20 points)
- EMA alignment (20 > 50 > 200)
- Distance from support (good entry?)
- Volatility (clean trend?)

### 4. Volume (0-15 points)
- Recent volume vs average
- Volume on up days vs down days
- Institutional buying?

### 5. Risk/Reward (0-10 points)
- Distance from recent highs
- Current drawdown
- Entry point quality

**Excellent:** 70+ points  
**Good:** 60-70 points  
**Acceptable:** 50-60 points  
**Pass:** <50 points
